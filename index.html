A Friend to Share With

Gigapet is unlike any other virtual pet. It's a companion who shares our food and nutrients, growing alongside us. Let's face it, kids are picky, and meal times can be stressful parts of our lives, but it doesn't have to be! With Gigapet we envisioned an interactive solution which shows us the immediate benefits and consequences of what we eat. It's not just a pet for our child to care for, it's an experience that connects us all with the common feeling of nurture.

How it Works

Choose

Pick from our list of wild animals ranging from adorable to ferocious

Feed

Feed your new friend whenever you eat and watch it grow

Track

Track your progress and daily intake and view them on our charts generated just for you
