Our Mission

We are a quality obsessed team. From Gigapet's conception, the team has always held our User experience in the core of our philosophy and design. Our founder was first inspired with this vision from the day they bought their child a Tomogatchi. He was amazed with the amount of connection a kid was able to have with a simple game, and wanted to take that further. With mobile technology today, it's finally possible to make a more complete virtual pet experience that takes that same sense of connection to a higher level and make it entwined to our own health, nutrition, and happiness.
